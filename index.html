SORCER STRIKER
ABOUT THE GAME

  • Sorcer Striker is a 1993 vertically scrolling shooter arcade video game developed by Raizing and published in 1993 by Able Corporation in Japan and Europe. In the game, players assume the role from one of the four bounty hunters to overthrow the Goblin empire led by King Gobligan and reclaim the bounty placed by King Codwenna of Violent Kingdom over Gobligan's head.

  • Defeat Enemies, engage in fast-paced battles with various types of dark creatures. Aim your spells accurately and use your special abilities wisely to defeat them. Complete quests, accept quests from NPCs throughout the realm. Follow the quest markers and accomplish objectives to progress in the game. Upgrade and Customize, collecting magical items and earning experience points to level up your sorcerer. Unlock powerful spells, enhance your abilities, and customize your character's appearance.

    HOW TO PLAY

    PRE-INTRO:

    To skip -> With keyboard = SPACE | Gamepad = A


    INTRO:

    To skip-> With keyboard = SPACE | Gamepad = A


    START:

    To add coins -> With keyboard = C | Gamepad = Start

    To skip -> With keyboard= SPACE | Gamepad = A


    PLAYER SELECTION SCREEN:

    To advance-> With keyboard = SPACE | Gamepad = A


    GAMEPLAY:

    Player:


    Movement:

    With keyboard= W,S,D,A | Gamepad = left joystick


    Attack:

    Bombs -> With keyboard = B | Gamepad = X

    Laser -> With keyboard = SPACE | Gamepad = A


    Enemies spawner:

    Blue chest -> With keyboard = 3 | Gamepad = right directional pad

    Green cheset -> With keyboard = 2 | Gamepad = down right directional pad

    Red chest -> With keyboard = 1 | Gamepad = up directional pad

    Power-up angel-> With keyboard = 4 | Gamepad = left directional pad

    Boss -> With keyboard = Q | Gamepad = Y


    Teleport -> With keyboard = T | Gamepad = B


    Modes:

    Insta Win -> With keyboard = F3 | Gamepad = L2

    Insta Lose -> With keyboard = F4 | Gamepad = R2

    Godmode -> With keyboard = F1 | Gamepad = L1

    See the colliders -> With keyboard = Q | Gamepad = R1
